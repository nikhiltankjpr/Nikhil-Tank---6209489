1947–48 Indo-Pak War

First Kashmir War

Operation & Conflict Overview

The Indo-Pak War of 1947–48 was the first armed conflict between India and Pakistan following independence. The war began in October 1947 after tribal forces from Pakistan invaded the princely state of Jammu and Kashmir.

Indian forces were airlifted into Srinagar to defend the region after the Instrument of Accession was signed. The conflict resulted in a ceasefire brokered by the United Nations in January 1948, dividing Kashmir along the Line of Control.
